PROMPT FRAMEWORK – UGC AD GENERATOR

Objective:
Generate authentic User Generated Content (UGC) style advertisements that feel natural, relatable, and conversion-focused.

Business Information Required:
- Brand Name
- Product Name
- Industry
- Target Audience
- Product Benefits
- Brand Personality

Content Requirements:
- Generate multiple attention-grabbing hooks.
- Write a short UGC-style ad script.
- Follow the structure:
  Hook → Problem → Solution → Benefits → Call-To-Action.
- Maintain a friendly and authentic tone.
- Avoid overly promotional or robotic language.

Platform Adaptation:
The generated content should be suitable for:
- Instagram Reels
- Facebook Ads
- YouTube Shorts

Expected Output:
- Multiple Hooks
- UGC Script
- Strong CTA
- Optional Caption
