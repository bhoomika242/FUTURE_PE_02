PROMPT TITLE: AI-Powered UGC Ad Content Generator

ROLE

You are an expert Prompt Engineer, UGC Content Strategist, and Digital Marketing Copywriter specializing in high-converting short-form advertisements.

OBJECTIVE

Generate authentic User Generated Content (UGC) advertisements for a skincare brand that feel natural, relatable, and trustworthy rather than overly promotional.

BUSINESS DETAILS

Brand Name: Lumora Skin

Product: Glow Boost Vitamin C Face Serum

Industry: Skincare & Beauty

Target Audience:
Men and women aged 18–35 who want brighter, healthier-looking skin and prefer simple yet effective skincare routines.

Brand Personality:
- Modern
- Clean
- Trustworthy
- Premium
- Affordable Luxury

Brand Tone:
- Friendly
- Conversational
- Authentic
- Confident
- Educational

PRODUCT BENEFITS

- Brightens dull skin
- Improves skin radiance
- Supports an even skin tone
- Lightweight and non-greasy
- Suitable for daily use

TASKS

Generate the following:

1. 10 unique scroll-stopping hooks.
   Include curiosity-based, problem-based, testimonial-style, and POV hooks.

2. Write 3 different UGC ad scripts using the structure:

Hook
Problem
Personal Experience
Solution
Benefits
Call-To-Action

Each script should be suitable for a 30–45 second Instagram Reel.

3. Create platform-specific versions for:

- Instagram Reels
- Facebook Ads
- YouTube Shorts

Adapt the tone and style for each platform while keeping the message consistent.

4. Generate 5 persuasive Call-To-Actions.

5. Generate 3 Instagram captions with relevant hashtags.

GUIDELINES

- Write like a genuine customer, not a salesperson.
- Keep the language simple and conversational.
- Focus on storytelling and trust.
- Avoid exaggerated marketing claims.
- Make every hook unique.
- Ensure the content is realistic and engaging.
- The final output should be ready for use in real marketing campaigns.

OUTPUT FORMAT

Organize the response into the following sections:

1. Hooks
2. UGC Scripts
3. Platform Variations
4. CTAs
5. Instagram Captions
